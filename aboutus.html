About US

More Than Shopping,It is Where Life Happens

Since opening our doors in 2011, Helwan Mall has been the premier shopping and entertainment destination in the region. What started as a vision to create a world-class retail experience has grown into a beloved community hub that welcomes over 50,000 visitors daily.

We pride ourselves on offering an unparalleled mix of international luxury brands, local favorites , exceptional dining options, and family-friendly entertainment—all under one roof. Our commitment to excellence has made us not just a shopping mall, but a lifestyle destination.
